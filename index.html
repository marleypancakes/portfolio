About Me

I am a 27-year-old software developer and transgender woman living in Brooklyn, NY. My interests include DJing, streaming games, playing music, writing, and doing mutual aid and harm reduction work in my community. In 2018 I graduated from University of California Santa Barbara with a Bachelor's of Arts in English. During my time at UCSB, I also studied physics, performance art and creative writing. For the next three years I have been working as a freelance writer and editor. My work has been published in places like the Daily Nexus, Matchbox Magazine, and Adilsons.org.


After deciding to pursue software development as a career, I spent the next two years working on a CRM application that nonprofits use to manage their donor data. In addition, I have built and maintained Writ.js, a browser extension that displays edits to online news articles in real-time.


Love for my community and a passion for new technologies are what motivates me. I am intrigued by the possibilities of using the internet to bring people together in new and interesting ways.
