About Me

I am a 24-year-old transgender woman living in Boston, MA but will be relocating to Brooklyn, NYC in the near future. My interests include virtual reality technologies, streaming games, playing music, writing, making things pretty, and doing mutual aid and harm reduction work in my community. In 2018 I graduated from University of California Santa Barbara with a Bachelor's of Arts in English. During my time at UCSB, I also studied physics, performance art and creative writing. For the last three years I have been working as a freelance writer and editor. My work has been published in places like the Daily Nexus, Matchbox Magazine, and Adilsons.org.


Love for my community and a passion for new technologies are what motivates me. I am intrigued by the possibilities of using the internet to bring people together in new and interesting ways.
